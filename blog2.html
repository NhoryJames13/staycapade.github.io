Kolsstaðir - Piece of Heaven

In search of an off-the-beaten-path experience, I embarked on a journey to Kolsstaðir, a secluded cottage nestled in the mountains of west Iceland. The drive alone was an adventure, as I meandered through picturesque landscapes and felt the thrill of anticipation building within me.

Upon arriving at Kolsstaðir, I was immediately struck by the untouched natural beauty that surrounded me. The mountains stretched as far as the eye could see, and the air carried a sense of tranquility that is hard to find elsewhere. This was truly a piece of heaven on Earth.

During my stay, I immersed myself in the beauty of the Icelandic countryside. I embarked on breathtaking hikes, following centuries-old sheep trails that crisscrossed the mountains. Each step brought me closer to nature, and the panoramic views of the ocean and surrounding mountains took my breath away.

As the day turned to night, I found myself gazing at the sky in hopes of catching a glimpse of the Northern lights. Luck was on my side, and the dancing lights illuminated the darkness, creating a spectacle that left me in awe of the wonders of the universe.

To truly experience the local culture, I visited the nearby village of Buðardalur. There, I had the opportunity to connect with the friendly locals, browse through local handicraft stores, and indulge in delicious Icelandic cuisine.

One of the highlights of my stay was a visit to the natural pool in Laugar, Sælingsdal. Surrounded by stunning natural beauty, the warm geothermal waters offered a soothing retreat for both my body and soul. I couldn't help but let the worries of the world melt away as I soaked in the rejuvenating waters.

Leaving Kolsstaðir was bittersweet. The memories of the untouched landscapes, the exhilarating hikes, and the tranquility of the mountains will forever be etched in my heart. Kolsstaðir had given me a glimpse into the raw beauty of Iceland and left me yearning for more adventures in this captivating country.
