Your questions, answered

Staycapade is an online home-sharing platform that connects hosts (homeowners or renters) with guests (tourists or travelers) seeking short-term accommodation in residential properties.

Staycapade enforces a fixed 4-96 commission rule, wherein the company will receive a 4% commission fee for every booking in this platform. Hosts, on the other hand, will receive 96% of booking earnings.

To become a host, you must avail a host membership. With this membership, you can list as many properties as you'd like. You must submit a host application with required requirements.

When signing up, both travellers and hosts are required to provide their personal information and submit requirements such as identification cards and other documents.
